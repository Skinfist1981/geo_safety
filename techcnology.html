Advanced Biometric Solutions

Our Biometric Solutions Are Changing the World

GEOValid® incorporates voice recognition, fingerprint technology, biometric information such as heartbeat, retinal scanning technology and other advanced emerging technologies and systems that empower organizations, large and small, with the ability to recognize any individual beyond any doubt.

  • Intel's Facial Recognition

    GEOValid® implements Intel’s facial recognition which is thought to be an information pool for the entire world. Our solutions now make the identifying and verifying of the individuals even easier with this new technology-in-progress.

  • Heartbeat Recognition

    If you cannot remember all of your passwords in your head, your heart can do that. The heart recognition technology is advancing, and GEOValid® is one of the first companies giving its users the chance to use their heartbeat as a PIN and password, thus increasing their protection and safety.

  • Voice Recognition

    As an advanced technology company, GEOValid® integrates the voice recognition technology in its solutions which gives its users total control and safety by simply using their voice. For example, think about the possibility to start the app and give direction for its use, using your own voice.

  • Fingerprint Identification

    The fingerprint identification is widely used in many governmental institutions and other secured facilities. Since GEOValid® works closely with many banks and other institutions, the fingerprint identification is used to identify and verify the individuals.

  • Password Recognition

    Of course - we have user name and password system that is 100% secure. All information that passes within any of the GeoValid systems is encrypted using MD5, SHA1, 256 or even up to 1024 - the same encryption used by Obama.

  • Advanced GPS Proprietary technology

    GEOValid® has a number of patents pending for the use of GPS coordinates when tracking.

  • Advanced Augmented Reality Technology

    GEOValid® has been pushing the limits of Augmented Reality since the iPhone was first introduced in 2007. We created the Paris Metro, Tokyo Metro, London Tube and numerous other public transport systems using AR technologies. GEOValid's use of AR coupled with Google Street is revolutionary.

  • Street Mapping and Location Technologies

    While Google pioneered street mapping, GEOValid® has remained at the forefront of this mapping technology - which empowers any GEOValid® user with the ability to take advantage of other street and identification mapping technologies that expand upon what Google first began.

  • Geofencing Technologies

    As a part of the new, technological world, GEOValid® implements the GEO Fencing feature in its solutions which empowers the users to set safe and dangerous zones. Imagine that you have a child and want to get informed every time he/she visits some dangerous place or even better set those zones as NO-GO – that’s what GEOValid® offers.

  • GEOValid Mobile Payment

    GEOValid® has been working within the banking industry since the inception of the iPhone regarding payment options. Our advanced understanding payment systems provide the vehicle for futuristic payment processing.

  • iPack

    iGEOValid®'s iPack technology empowers parents with the ability to keep track of their children who aren't old enough to carry a phone. The iPack has built-in GPS, stereo, multiple USB ports, WIFI and "nuclear" powered battery.

  • iTrax GPS tracking system

    iTrax GPS tracking system includes a GPS signaling device that is no more than a silver dollar in size. It empowers our users to track anything that can be moved.
